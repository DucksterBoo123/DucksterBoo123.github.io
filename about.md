# About Me
<font size="4">
I am an 18 year old student who is currently studying Maths, Physics and Computer Science for my A-Levels of which I will be sitting the exams for in May 2024. I have aspirations of becoming a pilot for British Airways alongside setting up a successful small indepent 3d graphics and design studio. 
<br>
<br>
Since the age of 4, my father has taught me everything to know about aviation, including the logistics and the hobby that is plane spotting. My favourite aircraft is the Boeing 777-200ER, which served at BA for their BA109/BA108 flights to and from Dubai. My father taught me the intricacies of plane spotting, from analysing the shape of a plane's wing to identifying the chassis specification just from the shape of the nose; all from unassisted visual observation from the window of our house.
<br>
<br>
Being the youngest sibling I have always had the influence of my two elder siblings influencing my tastes and preferences. This is most noticable in the music I listen to and create. One of my career goals is becoming a well established remix artist akin to Andy C, Lido and Fred Again.. (during his live shows). As a drummer, I have a developed understanding in the math behind music as well as the phyics behind the sounds that are made by real life instruments, such as stationary waves and how they are produced from strumming a gruitar. Overall, creating a synoptic link between a key hobby of mine and the subjects I have chosen to take further at A-Level.

I have always been interested in the complex mechanics of vehicles and comparing different properties of said vehicles has always been the most fun part to partake in. Of course I am talking about racing and motorsport; with both of my Uncles (one on my Mother's side and one on my Father's side) taking part in different forms of racing, (karting events, street racing, rally racing, etc) I have had great support from two very experienced drivers with regards to my involvement in the sport. I currently race in an F1 league on the racing simulator <em>Assetto Corsa</em> along with being an active member of the World Sim Series. Racing in a physically accurate simulator keeps my racing skills and mental racing state sharp for when I am out on track. Having recently obtained my FIA Race License, I wish to partake in a karting series after I complete my A-Levels during the summer break.
